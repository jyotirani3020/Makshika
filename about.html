About Us

Our goal

Makshika Organics takes pride in selling Authentic, Pure, Good Quality products within India. No Chemicals are added in our products. Our mission is our commitment to Quality. We are a proud Company that takes micro steps in ensuring the quality of honey, so you can trust the product is pure from hive to home

Who we are

We are a small and growing team of dedicated specialist with professional backgrounds based out of Delhi who carefully craft and nurture Makshika Honey & other Honey products. As a customer, you get only authentic Environmentally friendly product with highest Quality, Free Shipping and Money Back Guarantee as per our return policy, at the comfort of being Online.

We care

We produce and source all of our honey from Tribals and the Beekeepers where dedication, commitment to quality and attention to every detail are especially high and benchmarked. We are totally passionate about our hardworking bees and have a deep respect for the purity of the produce. We never compromise on the health of our bees. Once the honey makes it way to the packaging facilities, we put it through the series of tests to ensure the quality & purity standards.
