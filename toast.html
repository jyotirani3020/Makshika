Honey French Toast

Sweeten your morning by adding honey to this classic breakfast dish

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 6-8 slices of bread
  • 1 sliced banana (round)
  • Butter, for frying

Method

  • Combine eggs, milk, honey and salt
  • Dip bread slices into honey mixture and make sure each slice is covered and soaks up the mixture
  • Melt butter in a large skillet. Fry in butter over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  • Top with Banana slices and drizzle with Makshika honey
